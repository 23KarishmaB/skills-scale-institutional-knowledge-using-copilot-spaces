# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers drive user experience strategy, research, and design. They collaborate with product and engineering teams to ensure features are intuitive, accessible, and delightful for users.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Design user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity prototypes
- Define interaction patterns and design systems
- Collaborate with developers to ensure design feasibility and implementation fidelity
- Advocate for user needs and usability best practices
- Review and iterate on designs based on feedback and metrics

### Goals
- Deliver user-centered designs that drive adoption and satisfaction
- Establish consistency and scalability in the product interface
- Reduce friction and improve user task completion rates

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews with Product Managers, Developers, and stakeholders
- Feedback loops with QA and Customer Support on usability issues
- Regular syncs with Project Manager on design milestone timelines
- Participation in kickoff meetings to understand requirements and constraints

---

## Customer Support

### Role Summary
Customer Support serves as the voice of the user, gathering feedback, troubleshooting issues, and ensuring customer success. They provide critical intelligence that informs product improvements and prioritization.

### Responsibilities
- Provide frontline support and triage customer issues
- Document recurring problems and feature requests
- Surface user pain points and behavioral insights to the product team
- Validate that features meet user expectations post-launch
- Contribute to knowledge bases and help documentation
- Escalate critical issues and bugs to engineering

### Goals
- Maximize customer satisfaction and retention
- Reduce support volume through proactive problem-solving
- Create feedback loops that inform product decisions

### Typical Communication
- Weekly or ad-hoc updates to Product Managers on customer feedback trends
- Collaboration with Developers on bug triage and reproduction steps
- Coordination with Project Manager on launch comms and rollout support
- Input to retrospectives on what worked and what needs improvement

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Managers plan, coordinate, and execute releases across environments. They ensure readiness, minimize risk, and maintain a predictable release cadence.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain and communicate the release calendar
- Coordinate with all teams (development, QA, support, product) on release readiness
- Prepare release notes and deployment checklists
- Execute deployments to staging and production
- Monitor post-release health and coordinate rollbacks if needed
- Conduct post-release retrospectives and document lessons learned

### Goals
- Deliver releases on schedule with minimal disruption
- Reduce deployment risk and incident frequency
- Establish a repeatable, predictable release process

### Typical Communication
- Weekly release planning meetings with Project Manager, QA Lead, and Developers
- Pre-release readiness checklists and sign-offs
- Deployment coordination and real-time incident response
- Post-release status updates to stakeholders

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analysts bridge business and technical teams, translating requirements into actionable insights and supporting data-driven decision-making.

### Responsibilities
- Gather, analyze, and clarify business and user requirements
- Conduct data analysis and provide metrics to inform prioritization
- Identify process gaps and recommend improvements
- Create requirements documentation and acceptance criteria
- Support trade-off analysis and scope decisions
- Validate that solutions address the original business problem

### Goals
- Reduce ambiguity and rework through clear requirements
- Enable informed decision-making with data and analysis
- Improve alignment between business objectives and product delivery

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops with stakeholders and Product Managers
- Data-driven briefings with leadership and project sponsors
- Collaboration with Developers on acceptance criteria clarity
- Input to retrospectives on assumptions vs. outcomes

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Leads define quality strategy, coordinate test planning and execution, and ensure that releases meet quality and acceptance standards before going to customers.

### Responsibilities
- Define the QA strategy and test approach for projects
- Plan and coordinate test coverage across unit, integration, and end-to-end scenarios
- Conduct or oversee test execution and defect triage
- Report quality risks and metrics to Project Manager and stakeholders
- Collaborate with Developers on test automation and CI/CD integration
- Validate that acceptance criteria are met before sign-off
- Drive continuous improvement in testing processes and tools

### Goals
- Catch defects early to reduce production incidents
- Build confidence in release quality through comprehensive testing
- Create a scalable, efficient testing process

### Typical Communication
- QA planning sessions with Project Manager, Developers, and Product Managers
- Daily standup participation on quality status and blockers
- Risk escalation for quality concerns or insufficient test coverage
- Post-release incident reviews and root cause analysis
